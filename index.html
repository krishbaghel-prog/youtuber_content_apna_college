Apna College



About Apna College :

Apna College is a popular Indian ed-tech (education technology) platform that provides coding, placement preparation, and technical education mainly through YouTube and paid online courses. It is especially famous among B.Tech / IT / CS students. It is not a real college (no degree is given), but an online learning platform.


History & Founding

  • Founded : 2020
  • Headquarters : Delhi, India
  • Founders :
    • Aman Dhattarwal
    • Shradha Khapra

Background Story :

  • Before Apna College, Aman Dhattarwal already had a strong YouTube presence (since 2015).
  • The platform was created during the COVID-19 era, when online education demand increased.
  • The aim was to provide affordable and accessible coding education to Indian students.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission:
    • To make quality tech education accessible to everyone
    • To help students crack coding interviews and get jobs
  • Vision:
    • Build a large community of developers
    • Provide practical, industry-ready skills

What Apna College Offers

  1. YouTube Content
    • Free tutorials on:
      • C++
      • Java
      • DSA (Data Structures & Algorithms)
      • Web Development
      • Machine Learning basics

    They have millions of subscribers and videos covering technical topics.

  2. Paid Courses / Batches
    • Some popular programs:
      • Alpha Batch (DSA)
      • Delta Batch (Web Dev)
      • Sigma Batch (Full Stack)
    • These include:
      • Recorded + live classes
      • Assignments
      • Projects
      • Placement preparation
  3. Placement Preparation
    • Resume building
    • Interview questions
    • Mock interviews
    • Roadmaps for tech jobs
  4. Community
    • Large student community
    • Doubt-solving groups
    • Peer learning

    They have built a community of millions of learners online.

Key Features / Specialities

  1. Beginner-Friendly Teaching
    • Explains concepts from zero level
    • Good for first-year / second-year students
  2. Focus on Coding + Jobs
    • Strong emphasis on:
      • DSA
      • Web development
      • Placement skills
  3. Practical Learning
    • Projects included
    • Real-world examples
  4. Affordable Education
    • Cheaper than many private institutes (Thats the best thing......)
  5. Massive Online Reach
    • Millions of students across India

Growth & Achievements

  • Rapid growth after 2020
  • One of the largest coding education YouTube platforms in India
  • Thousands of students reportedly benefited in placements.

Advantages & Disadvantages

  • Advantages :
    • Free content available
    • Structured learning paths
    • Good for beginners
    • Active community
  • Disadvantages :
    • Not a real college (no degree)
    • Some content is basic-level
    • Paid courses may not suit everyone

Who Should Use Apna College?

  • Best for :
    • Beginners in coding
    • B.Tech / IT students
    • Students preparing for placements
  • Not ideal for :
    • Advanced developers only
    • People expecting a formal degree

Overall

Apna College is a modern online learning platform that has played a big role in helping Indian students learn coding and prepare for tech jobs. While it is not a traditional college, its affordable courses, beginner-friendly teaching, and strong online presence make it very popular among engineering students.
